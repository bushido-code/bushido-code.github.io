Bushido Code is the interview bootcamp that prepares Stanford students to get technical internships and full-time jobs. Come back to Stanford a week early and you'll master the coding interview skills not taught at school. You'll have daily lectures and one-on-one interviews, all while living in a beautiful Silicon Valley villa with friends.
